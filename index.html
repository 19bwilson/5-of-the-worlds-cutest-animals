There are a ton of cute animals out in the internet, here are some of the cutest.

Proboscis Monkey

Proboscis Monkeys large nose allows the males to make loud bellowing noises to warn about predotors. They are only found in the island of Borneo in southern Asia. They are often found in trees of swamps, or sometimes swimming around.

Aye-Aye Lemur

They are the biggest nocturnal primate and very distant cousins to humans, they are also the only primate to be able to use echolocation.

Red Lipped BatFish

These little guys are commonly found in the deep waters of the Galapagos islands and a bit off Peru in depths of 3 to 76m. They aren't really good swimmers so they have learn't to walk on their dorsal fins.

Star-Nosed Mole

These boys are the fastest eaters in the mammal kingdom, they can identify and eat their main source of food in less than two-tenths of a second.

Blob Fish

This bloby boy inhabits the waters of Austrila, Tasmaina and New Zealand, they can grow up to 30cms and live in between 600- 1,200m in the water
